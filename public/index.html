Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026
Short version: Your documents are processed to generate results and then deleted. We do not sell your data. We do not use your files to train AI models.

1. What data we collect

When you use PDFClarify, we collect:

  • Your email address — only if you create an account (used for login and billing).
  • Uploaded PDF files — temporarily stored in encrypted cloud storage (Cloudflare R2) for the duration of processing, then deleted.
  • Analysis results — the structured output (summary, actions, risks) saved to your account history if you are signed in.
  • Basic usage metadata — document count, page count, timestamps. Never file contents in logs.

2. How we process your documents

Your PDF is sent to the Google Vertex AI API (Gemini 2.5 Flash model) for analysis. We use Vertex AI — not Google AI Studio — which means:

  • Google does not use your document contents to train its models.
  • Data is not retained by Google after the API response is returned.
  • All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3.
  • Google's processing is governed by a signed Data Processing Agreement (DPA) under GDPR.

3. Data retention

  • PDF files: Deleted from storage immediately after analysis is complete.
  • Analysis results: Stored in your account history. You can delete them at any time from your dashboard.
  • Accounts: Deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google Vertex AI — for document analysis (see Section 2).
  • Cloudflare — infrastructure provider (storage, hosting, database). Cloudflare's DPA covers GDPR compliance.
  • Stripe / Lemon Squeezy — payment processing only (email and billing data, no document contents).

5. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU or EEA, you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@pdfclarify.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use only essential session cookies for authentication. No tracking cookies, no third-party advertising pixels.

7. Changes to this policy

We will notify registered users by email if we make material changes to this policy.

8. Contact

Questions? Email us at privacy@pdfclarify.com.
