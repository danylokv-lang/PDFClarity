Executive Summary

The Q4 Strategy Report outlines the company's expansion into three new European markets — Germany, France, and Poland — with a projected investment of €4.2M over two fiscal years. Revenue growth targets have been revised upward to 34% YoY, underpinned by the successful Series B close and the integration of the Klara AI acquisition.

The product roadmap prioritizes mobile-first UX improvements and API monetization, with enterprise tier launches scheduled for Q2 2026. The report recommends a restructured engineering org with two new pods focused exclusively on platform stability and compliance.

Operational efficiency gains from the recent AWS migration are projected to reduce infra costs by 18% annually, partially offsetting headcount costs from the planned hiring of 12 senior engineers and 4 PMs.
